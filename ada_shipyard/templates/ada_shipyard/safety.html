Health, Safety and Environment

Occupational health and safety is the highest priority of Ada Shipyard and is strictly observed. All work is secured with top-level security protocols and risk analysis. Quality and efficient production is the way to create a safe and healthy environment. For this reason, the Ada Shipyard takes care of the health and safety of its employees in the business environment and prevents the occurrence of job accidents. In order to provide these, Ada Shipyard has ISO 9001: 2008 Quality Management Standard, ISO 14001: 2004 Environmental Management International Standard and OHSAS 18001: 2007 Occupational Health and Safety Standard.

Our Goals

  • Secure work tools and machines
  • Minimize the risks and prevent injuries and injuries
