Our Quality Policy

{% endblock header %} {% block content%}
  1. Create sense of belonging and team spirit in employees
  2. Design and plan projects as detailed as possible in advance using related software and to keep all projects under control
  3. Be transparent and open to clients, to ensure that they can inspect their projects as they see fit
  4. Provide an effective quality control system with a clear, just and measurable structure and to keep quality at the top level
  5. Encourage all employees to feedback, ensuring continuous improvement with a good feedback system
  6. Use an integrated system to ensure that all internal and external service personnel working within the organization work in harmony
  7. Prevent accidents in safety, health and environment
  8. Work with relevant local, national and international laws and requirements
  9. Create the design and production standards of the shipyard, accelerating both the design and the production and increase the productivity
  10. Providing early production in order to lower the cost of the vessels and improve quality
  11. Make production with "design for production" philosophy in order to facilitate design and production
  12. Make contracts and major construction plans that do not exceed limits of the shipyard
  13. Create physical and virtual environments that shows the vital importance of material control
